The Begining

The Fresherwells journey began in 2016 when two college friends, John Salivan and Kris Klopperman got together and started selling IT services of websites, mobile app & digital marketing.

Mission

Our goal at Fresherwells is to combine the strength of a nationwide network with the individualised care of regional IT providers. We think that's easier said than done. Our offices provide our clients with high-quality, personalised IT services from highly skilled local team members.

We are global

Our national network allows us to offer best-in-class services like managed IT services, cloud services, digital marketing, custom software, specialised security and compliance advisory services, a 24/7 help desk, and more.

Quality Product

As the top provider of IT service solutions in the UK, we start by developing an MVP to gauge how well your product works. We make sure that thorough testing procedures are followed, that we get ongoing input to satisfy user needs, and that the program is simple to use and intuitive.

Quick development

As a reputable provider of software solutions, our team optimizes all procedures and uses cutting-edge agile techniques to provide mobile software development services. This results in a 50% reduction in the time needed to develop custom software applications.

Latest Technology

Complete Transparency

Transparency is a prerequisite for successful business partnerships. As an outsourcing provider for software development, we always give our clients an accurate picture of the progress of their projects and an explanation of every technological choice.

People
You deserve the best. We are the finest. To put things simply, we care — every single one of us, from the account managers to the technicians. We choose, develop, and employ the best personnel who are committed to prioritising your requirements.
