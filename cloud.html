Design, Advisory & Consultancy
  • Learn about your current IT and application environment.

    We'll work together to develop a legacy that exists within your IT estate. We will determine the flow of data and operations via your infrastructure, concentrating on the essential services and apps that keep your company operating

  • Create a cloud plan that suits your needs.

    We will assess your current organisational structure, internal capabilities, and cloud-related competencies. To assist you in achieving the intended results, we offer you a detailed plan of action that is based on industry best practices.

  • Let's plan your cloud migration

    We will offer clear advice about what parts of your estate can be moved to the cloud, what can’t be moved, and the best course of action to mitigate risk and maintain business as usual. There is no single right answer for everyone. The migration path needs to be built around your business goals.

  • Make an adoption plan and a business case

    Recognize the costs associated with migration, operations, schedules, and TCO for your cloud approach. With this amount of information at your disposal, you can create a strong business case and convince the rest of the organization to adopt the strategy.

Move, Migration & Adoption
  • Select the appropriate tactic

    "Lift and shift" is not quite well-known. If that is all you do, then well done. However, it might be the most effective tactic for particular workloads. It helps manage legacy applications and minimises disturbance. It could also be a first step toward a more all-encompassing cloud-native plan.

  • Go quickly to the cloud

    Frequently, a noteworthy occurrence has drawn attention to the shift to cloud computing. A licence renewal is approaching, a data centre that needs to be shuttered, etc. We are aware that some tasks have short deadlines and are urgent.

  • Oversee outdated applications and services.

    Fresherwells understand that not everything is ready for the cloud yet. We are able to supply all of the conventional services required to support this type of hybrid solution because of our history as a managed service provider in the data centre industry.

  • Provide significant cost savings

    Implementations of cloud computing that are not well optimised can be quite costly. We prioritise cost optimisation and application upgrading in order to attain optimal cloud economics.
