Our approach to mobile.
  • Develop a business case
    We can assist you with digital service conceptualisation and design, prioritising the areas of your process that would benefit from a mobile approach, and bolstering your business case with prototypes and proof of concept work.
  • Begin with your users
    Each of our mobile projects is based on careful user research, customer path mapping, and UX design.
  • Consider your whole digital estate
    How will your mobile experience be linked with other touchpoints? Do you need to integrate your web estate or CRM?
  • Cross-device compatibility
    Our native-first approach, which includes skilled iOS and Android engineers, offers a pleasant experience on either device. We test using a variety of physical devices from Apple and major Android OEMs.
  • Long term support
    Having an app implies you can now manage a digital product. Mobile technology and consumer expectations vary over time. We will work with you to develop a long-term support and technological upgrade strategy.
  • Secure implementations
    For the security testing of mobile apps, we use the OWASP Mobile Security Testing Guide (MSTG) and OWASP Mobile Application Security Verification Standard (MASVS) as guidelines for the creation of security-related test scripts. Furthermore, for clients in regulated industries, our application source code goes through independent third-party security audits.
